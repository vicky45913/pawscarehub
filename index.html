Saving Lives One Paw at a Time

At PawsCare Hub, every day we rescue abandoned, injured, and helpless dogs. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide medical treatment, food, shelter, and a chance at a better life.

So far, we've treated over 350 dogs and successfully helped 120+ dogs find loving homes. But the journey is far from over — your support keeps the hope alive.

About Us

Founded in 2020, PawsCare Hub is a passionate community of animal lovers and volunteers dedicated to animal welfare and rescue. We believe every dog deserves a second chance, regardless of age, breed, or condition.

Our mission is not just rescue but rehabilitation and responsible adoption, educating the community about compassionate pet ownership and prevention of cruelty.

Our Work & Services

  • Medical Treatment: Vaccinations, wound care, parasite control, surgeries, and emergency care.
  • Rehabilitation: Nutrition, shelter, and behavioral training to prepare dogs for adoption.
  • Adoption Facilitation: Connecting loving families with rescued dogs and thorough vetting.
  • Community Outreach: Awareness campaigns, school programs, and animal welfare workshops.
  • Emergency Rescue: Rapid response team for injured or abandoned dogs in distress.
