About PawsCare Hub

PawsCare Hub began in 2018 with a small group of animal lovers determined to save abandoned and injured dogs from the streets. Over the years, we have rescued hundreds, provided critical medical care, and found loving homes.

Our mission is to create a world where every dog is safe, healthy, and loved. With your support, we continue this journey of hope and healing.
