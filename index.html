About PawsCare Hub

Founded in 2018, PawsCare Hub started with a small team of passionate animal lovers who couldn’t ignore the plight of abandoned and injured dogs roaming the streets. Over the years, we have rescued hundreds of dogs, providing emergency medical care, shelter, and finding loving forever homes for them.

Our mission is to create a safer, healthier environment where every dog has a chance to live with dignity and love. We believe in transparency, compassion, and dedication to the cause of animal welfare.
